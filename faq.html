Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our visa services and application process

How long does the visa application process take?

+

Processing times vary depending on the type of visa and country. Tourist visas typically take 5-10 business days, while business and work visas may take 2-4 weeks. We provide estimated timelines during your consultation.

What documents do I need for my visa application?

+

Required documents vary by visa type and destination country. Generally, you'll need a valid passport, photographs, application forms, financial statements, and supporting documents. We provide a complete checklist during consultation.

Do you offer consultation services?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive consultation services to assess your needs and provide personalized guidance. Our experienced consultants will evaluate your case and recommend the best approach for your specific situation.

What are your service fees?

+

Our fees vary based on the type of service and complexity of your case. We provide transparent pricing during consultation with no hidden charges. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your needs.

Can you help with visa rejections or appeals?

+

Yes, we provide assistance with visa rejections and can help you understand the reasons for rejection. We also offer appeal services and guidance on reapplication strategies to improve your chances of success.

Do you assist with business setup and expatriate quotas?

+

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive business setup services including company formation, licensing, and expatriate quota management. Our team has extensive experience in helping businesses establish operations internationally.

Is my personal information secure with you?

+

Yes, we maintain strict confidentiality and security protocols for all client information. Your personal and business data is protected according to international privacy standards and is never shared with unauthorized parties.

Do you provide services for all countries?

+

We provide visa and immigration services for most countries worldwide. Our team has expertise in various international immigration systems and can assist with applications to multiple destinations. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
